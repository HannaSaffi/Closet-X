### 2. Digital Wardrobe Management

#### Adding Clothing Items

**Step-by-Step:**
1. Click **"Add Clothing"** from the navigation menu
2. Upload a photo of your clothing item
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP
   - Maximum size: 5MB
   - Best results: Clear photo with solid background
3. AI will automatically analyze your photo and suggest:
   - Category (tops, bottoms, shoes, accessories, outerwear)
   - Colors detected
   - Style type (casual, formal, athletic)
   - Suitable occasions

4. Review and edit the AI suggestions:
   - **Category:** Select from dropdown if AI got it wrong
   - **Primary Color:** Choose the main color
   - **Seasons:** Select which seasons this item works for
   - **Brand:** Optional - add brand name
   - **Purchase Info:** Optional - date and price
   - **Tags:** Add custom tags for easy searching

5. Click **"Save Item"** to add to your closet

**AI Features:**
- 🎨 **Color Detection:** Automatically identifies all colors in the garment
- 🏷️ **Smart Categorization:** 92%+ accuracy in determining item type
- ✨ **Background Removal:** Automatically removes photo backgrounds for clean look
- 🤖 **Style Recognition:** Identifies formal, casual, athletic, or business style

#### Viewing Your Wardrobe

**Closet View:**
- Navigate to **"My Closet"**
- See all your items in a grid layout
- Each card shows:
  - Item photo
  - Category
  - Primary color
  - Quick action buttons (Edit, Delete)

### 3. Outfit Recommendations

#### AI Style Chat - Daily Outfit Suggestions

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **"AI Style Chat"** from the menu
2. The AI assistant will greet you
3. Ask for outfit suggestions in natural language:

**Example Prompts:**
- "What should I wear today?"
- "I have a business meeting, suggest an outfit"
- "Give me a casual outfit for 65 degrees"
- "I'm going to a wedding, what should I wear?"
- "Comfortable outfit for working from home"

**How It Works:**
1. AI analyzes your prompt for:
   - Occasion (casual, business, formal, athletic)
   - Weather preferences
   - Style hints

2. Fetches real-time weather for your location
3. Searches your wardrobe for matching items
4. Applies algorithms:
   - **Color Harmony:** Ensures colors work well together
   - **Style Compatibility:** Matches formal with formal, casual with casual
   - **Weather Appropriateness:** Considers temperature and conditions

4. Generates multiple outfit combinations
5. Ranks them by composite score:
   - Color harmony: 30%
   - Style match: 40%
   - Weather appropriateness: 30%

**Features:**
- 🌤️ **Weather Integration:** Real-time data for your location
- 🎨 **Color Matching:** Complementary, analogous, and neutral harmony
- 🤖 **Smart Recommendations:** Indoor occasions skip outerwear automatically
- 💬 **Conversational:** Chat naturally - no rigid commands

**Outfit Display:**
- See 3-5 outfit suggestions
- Each outfit shows:
  - All clothing items with photos
  - Overall compatibility score
  - Weather details
  - "Save Outfit" button

#### Saving Favorite Outfits

1. When viewing outfit recommendations, click **"Save Outfit"**
2. Add optional notes (e.g., "Wedding outfit", "Work presentation")
3. Outfit is saved to "My Favorites"

#### Viewing Saved Outfits

1. Navigate to **"My Favorites"**
2. Browse all your saved outfits
3. Each shows:
   - All items in the outfit
   - When you saved it
   - Your notes
4. Click any outfit to view full details
5. Delete outfits you no longer want

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Getting the Best Outfit Recommendations

1. **Complete Your Wardrobe:**
   - Add at least 15-20 items for good variety
   - Include different categories (tops, bottoms, shoes, accessories)
   - Add items for different seasons

2. **Use Descriptive Prompts:**
   - ✅ "Casual Friday work outfit, 70 degrees"
   - ❌ "Outfit"

3. **Tag Your Items:**
   - Add tags like "work", "date night", "comfy", "fancy"
   - Makes filtering and recommendations more accurate

4. **Update Wear Frequency:**
   - Mark when you wear items
   - AI learns your preferences over time

### Photo Upload Best Practices

**For Best AI Recognition:**
1. ✅ **Good Lighting:** Natural daylight or bright room
2. ✅ **Flat Surface:** Lay clothing flat or hang neatly
3. ✅ **Full Item:** Capture entire garment in frame
4. ✅ **Simple Background:** Solid color background works best
5. ❌ **Avoid:** Wrinkled, bunched, or partially visible items

**Example Photo Setup:**
```
Good: White bed, clothing laid flat, well-lit room
Bad: Crumpled on floor, poor lighting, only half visible
```

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### Photo Upload Issues

**Problem:** "File too large" error
- **Solution:** Resize image to under 5MB
- Use tools like TinyPNG or ImageOptim

**Problem:** AI misidentifies item
- **Solution:** Manually correct the category and resubmit
- Ensure photo is clear and well-lit

### Outfit Recommendations

**Problem:** "Not enough items" message
- **Solution:** Add more items to your wardrobe (minimum 10 recommended)

**Problem:** Outfits don't match my style
- **Solution:**
  1. Update your style preferences in Settings
  2. Add more tags to your items
  3. Use more specific prompts ("professional business attire" vs "work")

### Login Issues

**Problem:** Forgot password
- **Solution:** Contact support or create new account
- Note: No automated password reset yet

**Problem:** Session expired
- **Solution:** Simply log in again
- Sessions last 7 days

## 🎯 Common Workflows

### Morning Routine: Getting Dressed

1. Open Closet-X on your phone
2. Navigate to **"AI Style Chat"**
3. Ask: "What should I wear today?"
4. Review weather-appropriate suggestions
5. Pick your favorite outfit
6. Optionally save it for future reference

**Time:** ~2 minutes

---

### Weekly Prep: Planning Outfits

1. Navigate to **"Weekly Forecast"**
2. See 7-day weather outlook
3. AI suggests outfits for each day
4. Save outfits you like
5. Know exactly what you're wearing all week

**Time:** ~10 minutes

---

### Closet Clean-Out: Finding What to Donate

1. Navigate to **"Analytics"**
2. Check **"Least Worn Items"**
3. Review items you haven't worn in 6+ months
4. Delete from wardrobe
5. Donate or sell in real life!

**Time:** ~15 minutes
